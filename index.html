The WarmHearth Kit is a DIY insulation pack for low-income Tasmanians needing affordable, renter-safe solutions to stay warm during winter.

The Problem

Tens of thousands of low-income households in Tasmania are forced to suffer during the winter in cold, poorly insulated homes, struggling to afford adequate heating. This ongoing issue known as energy poverty is especially concentrated in Hobart's northern suburbs and continually contributes to greater financial stress, lower quality of life, and poorer health for struggling Tasmanians.

Our Value Proposition and Solution

The WarmHearth Kit is for low-income Tasmanian households who are needing to stay warm during the winter without making expensive, permanent changes to their homes or increasing their energy bills. Our product is a do-it-yourself home insulation kit that provides affordable tools to reduce heat-loss and increase comfort in older, less energy efficient homes. Unlike the current unaffordable professional grade retrofits and complicated landlord-dependent building changes, the WarmHearth Kit gives Tasmanians usable, renter-safe solutions that they can install themselves to reduce financial stress and make their lives easier.

About Us
