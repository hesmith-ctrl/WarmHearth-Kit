WarmHearth Kit

Affordable Warmth for Tasmanian Homes

What’s in the Kit?

The WarmHearth Kit is a DIY insulation pack for low-income Tasmanians needing affordable, renter-safe solutions to stay warm during winter.

The Problem

Energy poverty affects 17% of Tasmanians. Cold climates, old homes, and rising bills leave many in Hobart’s northern suburbs at risk of health issues, stress, and discomfort.

Our Value Proposition

The WarmHearth Kit is for low-income Tasmanian households who are needing to stay warm during the winter without making expensive, permanent changes to their homes or increasing their energy bills. The WarmHearth Kit is a do-it-yourself home insulation kit that provides affordable tools to reduce heat-loss and increase comfort in older, less energy efficient homes. Unlike the current unaffordable professional grade retrofits and complicated landlord-dependent building changes, WarmHearth Kit gives Tasmanians usable, renter-safe solutions that they can install themselves to reduce financial stress and make their lives easier.

About Us

This project was created as part of the AFS Global STEM Innovators Program by a Hobart-based student passionate about sustainability, equity, and innovation.
